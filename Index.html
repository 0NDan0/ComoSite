COMO

COMO is a city in Lombardy, Italy. It is the administrative capital of the Province of Como.
Its proximity to Lake Como and to the Alps has made Como a tourist destination and the city contains numerous works of art, churches, gardens, museums,
theatres, parks and palaces: the Duomo, the Basilica of Sant'Abbondio, the Villa Olmo,
the public gardens with the Tempio Voltiano, the Teatro Sociale, the Broletto (the city's medieval town hall)
and the 20th century Casa del Fascio.
